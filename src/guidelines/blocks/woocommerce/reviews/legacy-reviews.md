# Legacy Review Blocks

**Status:** Older review blocks, being replaced by `woocommerce/product-reviews`
**React Component:** Not yet implemented (planned)

---

## Blocks

### All Reviews
- **Name:** `woocommerce/all-reviews`
- **Description:** Show a list of all product reviews.
- **Supports:** `color` (text, background), `interactivity`, `typography` (fontSize)
- **CSS:** `.wc-block-all-reviews`

### Reviews by Category
- **Name:** `woocommerce/reviews-by-category`
- **Description:** Show product reviews from specific categories.
- **Supports:** `color` (text, background), `interactivity`, `typography` (fontSize)
- **CSS:** `.wc-block-reviews-by-category`

### Reviews by Product
- **Name:** `woocommerce/reviews-by-product`
- **Description:** Display reviews for your products.
- **Supports:** `color` (text, background), `interactivity`, `typography` (fontSize)
- **CSS:** `.wc-block-reviews-by-product`

---

## Migration

These blocks are superseded by the modern `woocommerce/product-reviews` block which provides a template-based, customizable review display system with individual child blocks for each review element.
