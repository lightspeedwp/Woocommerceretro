# TeamGridSection Pattern

**Version:** 1.0
**Type:** Pattern (Team Display)
**File:** `/src/app/components/patterns/TeamGridSection.tsx`
**BEM:** `.wp-team-grid__*`

---

## Overview

Displays team members in a responsive card grid with photo, name, role, and optional social links. Used on about and team pages.

---

## Retro / funky spec

- Glow team cards on hover
- Gradient role text
- Glass card background
- Spring hover lift
- Neon social link icons

---

## Accessibility

- `<section aria-label="Our team">` wrapper
- Team member cards as `<article>` elements
- Social links: `aria-label="[Name] on [Platform]"`
